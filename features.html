both hardware and software.

Your money, in your hands

At Savvy, we prioritize the security of your financial data by employing both advanced hardware security systems and cutting-edge software protocols. We utilize biometric authentication and encryption technology to ensure that only you can access your account. Your money is always under your control, securely stored and easily accessible from your device.
Simple yet secure

We leverage AI-powered systems to monitor for unusual activity and prevent fraud. This intelligent security layer continuously analyzes your transactions in real-time, allowing Savvy to respond to threats quickly, enhancing the safety of every interaction you make. Our secure infrastructure ensures that your personal data is always protected.
Truly open source

Transparency is key to our approach. Savvy is built on open-source technologies, which means our security practices are open for review by the community. This allows us to stay ahead of potential vulnerabilities, with regular updates and patches applied to ensure a robust defense system that keeps your financial data secure.
